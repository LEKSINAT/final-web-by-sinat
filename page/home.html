Technology is the use of knowledge, tools, machines, and systems to solve problems and make life easier. It appears in many forms, from simple tools like hammers to advanced systems like computers and artificial intelligence. Technology plays a major role in our daily lives by improving communication through phones and the internet, saving time and effort with machines and automation, and advancing education through online learning and digital tools. It also improves healthcare with modern medical equipment and treatments, while supporting industries and transportation with efficient systems and machines. In short, technology helps people work smarter, connect faster, and live better.

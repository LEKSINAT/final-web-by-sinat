Passerellnumeriques Cambodia

Scholarships that teach about Information Technology (IT) are special programs designed to help students gain IT skills without paying full tuition fees. These scholarships cover training in areas such as programming, web development, networking, cybersecurity, and database management. Many institutions around the world, including in Cambodia, provide IT-focused scholarships such as those from TUX Global Institute, NTTI, ISTAD, and the Korea Software HRD Center. They often offer free or discounted tuition, learning materials, and sometimes even monthly stipends. These programs are aimed at students who show academic potential, have financial difficulties, or demonstrate a strong interest in technology. By joining such scholarships, students can build solid IT skills, earn internationally recognized certifications, and improve their chances of getting jobs in the growing technology sector.
